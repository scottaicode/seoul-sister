## 1. THE STRATEGIC CALL (read first — this is WHY the video is shaped this way)

**Do NOT make a "Hi, I'm Yuri" AI-spokesperson intro as the launch piece.** In June 2026 that format is the most saturated, most scroll-past thing on the internet — it leads with the *technology* and screams "AI product" to a skeptical Gen Z K-beauty native, which is a negative trust signal.

**Instead: a demonstration of real value.** Seoul Sister's advantage is that the value is *real and provable* — Bailey lived it for four months. So the video SHOWS Yuri doing what no competitor can (reading a skin photo, catching a wasted purchase, pausing actives for a menstrual cycle, refusing to vouch for an unverified retailer), and CLOSES on a real subscriber's real outcome.

**The three load-bearing principles:**
1. **Open on the viewer's problem, not the brand.** First 2 seconds = "is this even real? am I doing this right?" — their pain, not our logo.
2. **Honesty is the hook.** Every competitor sells you *more*. Yuri tells you to buy *less* ("skip the repurchase," "I can't vouch for that retailer"). In 2026, radical honesty IS the trust moat. Lead with it.
3. **End on earned proof, not a CTA scream.** Bailey's real words close it: *"my skin's the best it's been since we started."*

**Tone / vibe:** "your smartest friend texts you back." Intimate, mobile-first, screen-recorded realness in the glass-skin palette (soft pink / rose gold / glass blue, luminous, airy). NOT a glossy beauty commercial, NOT tech-bro "AI revolution," NOT influencer gloss. The brand voice is "expensive water" not "premium formulation" — the video must sound like that or it betrays the product.

**Biographical lock (inherited from the voice package, Option B):** Yuri is openly an honest AI advisor. No fake human backstory. Every later video inherits whatever this one establishes.

## 4. HEYGEN vs SCREEN-RECORDING — what gets made how

**This is a HYBRID. HeyGen is one ingredient, not the whole video.**

| Component | Technique | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Yuri's spoken lines (beats 2, 3, 7) | **HeyGen avatar** (locked portrait + ElevenLabs MP3 as custom audio) | Her face, lip-synced. The ONLY HeyGen footage. |
| The conversation proof (beats 2, 4, 5, 6) | **Screen recording** of the real app (lightly cleaned) | The unfakeable core. Record on a real phone/the PWA. |
| Cold open (beat 1) | B-roll (phone at mirror / shelf) — Nano Banana stills + light motion, or simple phone footage | |
| Captions | Burned-in, glass-skin type | Most watch muted — captions are mandatory. |
| Music | Soft single piano/synth pad, "morning light," low in mix | ElevenLabs Music or licensed. |
| End card | Logo + URL on glass gradient, hold 2s | |

**Assembly:** CapCut or Descript. Drop HeyGen clips + screen recordings + B-roll on a timeline, layer captions + music, export 9:16 and 16:9.

### The HeyGen mechanics (the non-negotiable workflow)
1. Render Yuri's lines (beats 2, 3, 7) in **ElevenLabs** — Voice = **V1**, Model = **Eleven v3**, Stability = **Natural**. End each with a trailing `...`. Spell "B.H.A." phonetically. Download MP3s.
2. **HeyGen → Avatars → Create New Avatar → upload photo** → upload Yuri's **locked portrait** (K-Beauty Insider #1).
3. Create video → select Yuri avatar → **"Upload Audio" (NOT text-to-speech)** → upload your ElevenLabs MP3. *(HeyGen's ElevenLabs API integration silently reverts to default settings and erases all tuning — ALWAYS upload the MP3, never let HeyGen generate audio. This is the #1 landmine.)*
4. Render 9:16 + 16:9 per clip.
5. If lipsync drifts on her resting mouth, do ONE Nano Banana image-edit of the master ("same woman, soft closed-lip resting mouth, facing camera") and re-rig.

## 6. WHAT TO AVOID (the anti-brief)

- ❌ A pure talking-head "Hi I'm Yuri" as the LAUNCH piece (saturated; leads with tech). Fine as the 15-sec landing loop, not the launch.
- ❌ Feature lists / widget names / "6 specialist agents" — important but boring on video.
- ❌ Glossy beauty-commercial gloss or "AI revolution" hype.
- ❌ Cutting the honesty beats (4a/4c) for runtime — they ARE the differentiator.
- ❌ Claiming subscriber/revenue numbers (there aren't any yet). The proof is Bailey's outcome + the honesty, not scale.
- ❌ Letting HeyGen generate the audio (erases voice tuning, no error).
