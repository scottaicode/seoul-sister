## Findings

### 🔴 P0 — Seasonal recommender survives on Scan + Product Detail (v10.8.9 incident #6 survivor)

**The seventh Yuri Sole Authority violation, found proactively before Bailey hit it.**

v10.8.9 killed the seasonal "SPRING TIP" (`texture_advice` "switch to lightweight
gel", `ingredients_to_emphasize` chips — a phase-blind `season + climate` →
`ss_learning_patterns` lookup) on the routine page and dashboard. The v10.8.9
changelog even wrote the lesson: *"grep `texture_advice`, `ingredients_to_emphasize`
across the whole src/ tree."* But the sweep missed two surfaces:

- `src/components/scan/ScanResults.tsx:315-316` renders `<SeasonalContext>`
- `src/components/products/ProductEnrichment.tsx:178-179` renders `<SeasonalContext>`
- Component: `SeasonalContext` in `src/components/shared/EnrichmentSections.tsx:397-439`
  — renders `textureAdvice` + "Good for this season" / **"Consider reducing this
  season"** ingredient chips.
- Data source: `fetchSeasonalContext()` in `src/lib/scanning/enrich-scan.ts:532-590`
  — pure `season + climate` lookup against `ss_learning_patterns`. Zero
  treatment-phase, decision-memory, or routine awareness.

When Bailey scans a product (or opens a product detail page) out of season, she
gets "switch to lightweight gel" and "Consider reducing [ingredient] this season"
— the exact phase-blind prescription class she's rejected six times.

**Fix**: Remove the `SeasonalContext` render from both `ScanResults.tsx` and
`ProductEnrichment.tsx`, and stop populating `seasonalContext` in `enrich-scan.ts`.
Keep all observational sections (price, community, counterfeit, trend, ingredient-
effectiveness data, overlap preview). The `ProductEnrichment` surface already has a
Yuri CTA for seasonal nuance. Mirrors the v10.8.9 + v10.8.10 fix exactly.

### 🟡 P2 — `ExpiringSection` still `sm:grid-cols-2` (v10.8.12 density-sweep survivor)

`src/components/library/ExpiringSection.tsx:73` uses
`grid grid-cols-1 sm:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3` with `ProductLibraryCard` — the
same card + breakpoint Bailey called "still squished" in v10.8.12, where
OwnedSection + SavedSection were fixed to single column. ExpiringSection is the lone
survivor. Mitigating: Expiring cards have no action buttons (less dense than Owned).
But the v10.8.12 precedent argues for consistency.

**Fix**: drop to `grid-cols-1 lg:grid-cols-2` so it never goes 2-up at the cramped
`sm` width (keep a 2-col option on genuinely wide screens since these cards are
sparse — don't need full single-column like Owned).

### 🟡 P2 — `ProductEnrichment` self-gates on one-shot `getSession()` (v10.8.11 race shape)

`src/components/products/ProductEnrichment.tsx:49-53` calls
`supabase.auth.getSession()` once in a useEffect to gate a "Sign in for personalized
intelligence" CTA vs the real enrichment. Currently SAFE by construction — its
parent `ProductIntelligenceSection` already gates via `useAuth()` (the v10.8.11 fix)
and only mounts `ProductEnrichment` after confirming the user is a subscriber, so
the session is hydrated by the time the inner `getSession()` fires. But it's fragile:
if anyone later renders `ProductEnrichment` standalone on a public route, the
v10.8.11 race reopens.

**Fix**: switch its auth detection to `useAuth()` for consistency and to make it
race-safe regardless of where it's mounted. Low risk, high consistency value.
