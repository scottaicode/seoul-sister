   • LGAAS blog + social      →                           →    [THE ONE METRIC reads here]

We have the **closer** (Yuri) and the **capture** (widget + email). We're missing **top-of-funnel volume**. Everything below targets that — then watches the conversion read.

## The ONE metric (inherited from North Star — do not invent a vanity metric)

**Visitor → paid conversion rate**, co-piloted by **count of real external paying subscribers.**
NOT views. NOT followers. NOT likes. Per `SOCIAL-VIDEO-ENGINE.md`: the teacher hierarchy is
**Yuri-conversations-started > profile/link clicks > saves/shares > views > likes.** A video with
50K views and 0 Yuri chats is a failure; a video with 800 views and 12 Yuri chats is a win.

**First job is still to make the number readable** — which means getting enough volume that a
conversion rate is even computable. At 23 visitors over 3 months, the denominator is too small to
mean anything. **First success milestone = 100+ real external visitors to Yuri in a 30-day window,
with the conversion rate visible.** Then move it.

## The 30-day first-traffic plan (assumes Bailey's gate opens)

### Week 1 — Launch the hero video + open the tap (small)
- **Bailey green-lights the "Meet Yuri" video** (8.6/10, locked, holding). She's home from the
  bachelorette ~June 21; give her a day, then a no-pressure ask.
- **Post the hero video** to TikTok + Reels + Shorts from a Seoul Sister account. Add a **trending
  sound** at post time per platform (LGAAS BP94 A4 — biggest free reach lever).
- **Reddit warm-link**: `glass_skin_atx` (837 karma, top 1%) does NOT spam links, but per the 90-day
  blueprint, route earned attention to the brand where rules allow (profile, DMs, allowed subs).
- **Confirm GA4 + funnel instrumentation is live** so every visitor from these is counted. (This is
  the make-the-metric-readable step — it is *permitted* work under the build freeze.)

### Week 2 — Bailey-fronted distribution + LGAAS baseline
- **Bailey starts the social cadence** (`SOCIAL-VIDEO-ENGINE.md`): 1 polished hero + 2–3 scrappy
  videos/week. Scrappy = real Yuri screen-recordings of a feature working (scan a label, catch a
  counterfeit, build a routine). She fronts these (her courses + authenticity = the asset).
- **LGAAS carries the floor**: Reddit discovery+response, blog generation grounded in the SS product
  DB, blog→social. This runs whether or not Bailey hits her cadence — the dependency floor.
- **Each video = ONE feature, ONE hook, ending in "ask Yuri."** Don't make feature-montages.

### Week 3 — Read the first signal, kill/double-down
- **Read the One Metric for the first time** with real volume. Which video drove Yuri chats? Which
  topic? Which platform?
- **Amplify the winner** (the `SOCIAL-VIDEO-ENGINE` discipline: Reddit/organic is the free A/B test;
  video amplifies ALREADY-validated winners). Make 2 more videos in the winning format/topic.
- **Look at the funnel drop-off**: of visitors who chatted with Yuri, how many hit the 20-msg wall?
  How many captured email? How many subscribed? The biggest drop = the next thing to fix
  (and *that* fix clears the build-freeze gate because it has a conversion hypothesis + metric).

### Week 4 — First conversion attempt + Bailey paid-social test
- **Goal: the first cold stranger pays $39.99.** If Yuri + email nurture haven't converted anyone,
  the drop-off data tells us where (price? trust? timing?). That becomes the first *validated* reason
  to change something.
- **Optional, Bailey-led**: a small **paid social test** ($50–100) on the best-performing organic
  video, using her ad-course training. Paid social is the fastest way to manufacture the traffic
  volume the metric needs — but only AFTER an organic winner proves the creative converts (don't pay
  to amplify an unproven video).

## Proof assets to collect (anonymized — the third-party-trust layer)

The strongest conversion proof Seoul Sister has is **real strangers thanking Yuri**, generated by hand
over months on Reddit (`glass_skin_atx`, Top 1% commenter, 1% poster on r/koreanskincare within ~1
month). Two documented corpora, both validated on a cold, AI-skeptical audience:

- **Public comments**: 1K+ upvote threads, multi-turn conversations strangers replied to 5+ times.
  See `REDDIT-VALIDATED-VIDEO-QUEUE.md` for the ones turned into a video shot list.
- **Private DMs** (even higher-value signal): strangers left the public thread, opened a DM, and came
  back over **days/weeks**. Yuri delivered the FULL phased protocol experience (Phase 1→2→3 barrier
  repair), **self-corrected across sessions** (walked back a low-pH vitamin C rec; conceded a
  not-FA-safe SPF to the user), and de-escalated buying anxiety (talked someone OUT of a worry). This
  is the authenticated-Yuri product happening for free in a DM — and the strangers loved it. It proves
  the funnel is **unmonetized, not unproven**: every one of these people would've been a trial sub if
  the conversation had been on seoulsister.com where the meter runs.

**The standout non-detection stat**: ~500 contributions, only **2 ever flagged as AI** (>99.6%), on
subs that mostly BAN AI (r/AsianBeauty rule 11, r/koreanskincare rule 4). This is the hard evidence
behind the launch video's proud-AI bet — Gen Z K-beauty natives didn't smell it, and when Yuri DID
disclose she's AI, the honesty was the asset.

**PRIVACY RULE (non-negotiable, same tier as protecting Bailey):** if any DM/comment becomes proof
material (social, landing page, decks), strip ALL real Reddit usernames and identifying details.
Attribute only as "a real subscriber" / "a stranger in the UK." Respect their privacy absolutely.

**Strategic discipline going forward** (so the moat-building doesn't cannibalize the funnel): the
free-help pattern should DELIVER real value, then point to Yuri for the deep personalized work
("ask Yuri to build your phases and adjust as your skin responds"). The DMs prove people want the deep
work; the business needs that deep work to happen where it can convert. Don't reproduce the full
phased protocol for free at scale — that's giving away the product, not running the funnel.
