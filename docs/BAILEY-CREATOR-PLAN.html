What the videos look like

Sixty seconds. Vertical. Your phone propped up somewhere. You're on your bed or at a table. No lighting setup, no set, no script.

You say the thing. If you're annoyed, you sound annoyed. If you wasted forty dollars, you say you wasted forty dollars.

If something's on screen, it's real: an actual ingredient list, an actual before and after, an actual receipt. Evidence, not decoration. A nicely designed graphic with a serif font gets scrolled past. A screenshot of the thing that actually broke you out gets sent to a friend.
